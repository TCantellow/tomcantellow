Resources for learning statistics and data science

Introductory Guides to R and Data Science:
  • R for Data Science – Hands-on guide to data science with R.
  • Just Enough R – Concise intro to R for beginners.
  • YaRrr! The Pirate’s Guide to R – Fun, accessible R programming guide.
  • Fundamentals of Data Visualisation – Comprehensive visualization concepts.
  • Introduction to Statistical Learning – Foundational machine learning textbook.
  • Statistical Rethinking – Key applied statistics textbook.
  • Statistical Rethinking Recoded – Recoded exercises from the book.
Advanced R and Hierarchical Modelling:
  • Beyond Multiple Linear Regression – Advanced regression techniques.
  • Models by Example – Practical statistical modeling examples.
  • Advanced R – Deep dive into R programming.
  • Applied Regression and Multilevel Models – Guide to regression and multilevel models.
  • Multilevel Model Overview – Introductory video on multilevel models.
  • Introduction to Multilevel Models – Tutorial for multilevel models in R.
  • Multilevel Model Specification – Detailed model specification post.
  • Multilevel Model Bootcamp – Comprehensive workshop material.
  • Nested Data Analysis – Video on nested data analysis.
  • Multilevel Models and Clustering – Guide on clustering methods.
  • Hierarchical Clustering on Categorical Data – Intro to hierarchical clustering in R.
  • Hierarchical Models in R – In-depth look at hierarchical models.
  • AllFit – Tool for evaluating multilevel models.
Geographic Data Science:
  • Mapping and Modelling Geographic Data in R – Mixed-methods spatial modeling.

  • Urban Analytics and City Science – Guide to urban data analysis.
  • Geographic Data Science – Intro to geographic data science in Python.
  • Geographic Data Science – Additional course on geographic data science.
  • Geocomputation with R – Advanced geospatial data analysis.
  • Spatial Microsimulation – Techniques in spatial microsimulation.
  • Esri GIS Blog – GIS and spatial analysis articles.
