Tom Cantellow

A Quantitative Human Geographer
Focusing on Politics, Economics, and Inequalities

I am a human geographer interested in understanding the politics and economy of Britain. I provide insight for researchers, policymakers, public stakeholders, and students.

I use spatial analysis and quantitative methodologies to analyse a range of topics, including inequality, elections, housing, politics, energy efficiency, personal finance, and local economies.

In 2024, I submitted my PhD in Human Geography at the University of Bristol, where I've also taught across a wide range of units. I have also worked with think tanks and the public sector at both national and local levels.

I am available to consult and collaborate on map production, data analysis, survey design, and many other topics. I'd love to chat about what you have in mind.
